Linus "Linux" Torvalds

The creator and main developer of the Linux kernel used by Linux and Android operating systems as well as Git, a distributed version control system.

Summary

Biography

Linus Benedicts Torvalds was born in 1969 on December 28th in Helsinki, Finland. His parents are Anna and Nils Torvalds and who was named after the American Nobel-Prize winning chemist, Linus Pauling. though he likes to claim it was after 'Linus' in the Peanuts comics.

Linux

As a Finnish software engineer he is most known for the development of the Linux kernel which was inspired by Minix. Linux is a Unix-like operating system that can on a PC but has grown to run on many other architectures to date. Only about 2% of the current Linux kernel is written by Linus, however despite that size of his contribution, he remains the ultimte authority on updates to the kernel.
